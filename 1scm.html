About Amity University Bangalore

Amity University Bangalore is a proud part of the **Amity Education Group**, India's leading education conglomerate with campuses across India and international locations. Established in 2010, our Bangalore campus spans over 60 acres of lush green landscape, boasting state-of-the-art infrastructure designed for modern learning.

We are officially recognized by the **University Grants Commission (UGC)** and proudly accredited by NAAC with an **'A' Grade**, a testament to our commitment to academic excellence. Amity Bangalore offers over 100 diverse programs across various disciplines, with a strong emphasis on cutting-edge research, innovation, and robust industry collaboration, ensuring our students are future-ready.

  • 60+ Acre High-Tech Campus: A sprawling environment for learning and growth.
  • 300+ Industry Collaborations: Bridging academia with real-world industry demands.
  • 100+ Academic Programs: A wide array of choices to shape your career path.
  • 500+ Expert Faculty Members: Dedicated educators and mentors guiding your journey.

Experience Vibrant Campus Life at Amity

Beyond academics, Amity University Bangalore offers a rich and engaging campus environment designed for holistic development.

Hostel Facilities

Comfortable and secure separate hostels for boys and girls, equipped with modern amenities, round-the-clock security, and recreational spaces for a home-like experience.

Sports Facilities

State-of-the-art sports infrastructure including an Olympic-size swimming pool, basketball courts, cricket ground, a well-equipped indoor sports complex, and a modern gymnasium to promote fitness and teamwork.

Central Library

Our expansive central library boasts over 200,000 books, 10,000+ e-journals, extensive digital resources, and quiet study spaces, fostering a conducive environment for research and learning.

Food Court & Cafeteria

A vibrant multi-cuisine food court offering a diverse menu of hygienic and delicious meal options, catering to varied tastes and preferences, ensuring students are well-nourished.
